When History Repeats Itself - I'm Getting a Dinosaur

Reptiles of the clade Dinosauria in our modern natural world

A unique chance to see dinosaurs in our new habitat. Featuring a guest apprenace by David Attenborough!

Open daily from 06 November 2023

11:00 - 17:00 (last entry 16:45)
Last day is 03 December 2023
Free admition for adults

Book tickets

Experience this highly anticipated event dedicated to dinosaurs in the real world.

Jurassic World has come to life as this four-week interactive exhibition aims to teach the science behind the reptiles that first appeared roughly 245 million years ago.

Come join us on this eventful show where the exhibitions will include interactive experiences such as games, virtual reality, and group activities for everybody to enjoy.

Sir David Attenborough will be making select guest appearances during the exhibition. For thease dates, visitors will require pre-booking tickets online to guarantee a place. Tickets are free for all however limited edition merchandise will be available in the gift shop.
